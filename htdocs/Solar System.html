Solar System

Our Home System

Our Solar System is one of the closest thing we can observe from Earth. Our Solar System is named after our Sun, Sol, which is named from the Latin word for Sun, "solis," and anything linked to the Sun is called "solar." This includes the inner planets( Mercury, Venus, Earth and Mars),the outer planets(Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus and Neptune),the dwarf planets(Pluto, Ceres, Eris, Haumea and Make-Make), the asteroid belts, the many meteroids, the many comets that past the Solar System and the Oopart Cloud . Spacecraft is also used to explore and discover more about the other planets in the Solar System besides Earth, to study other celestial bodies like asteroids and meteorites, to see faraway stars and exoplanets and do many other things.
