Stars

The Light of the Night Sky

The stars are what makes and lights up the night sky, they are all dotted around the celestial sphere with our moon. Only a small proportion of the observable universe's tens of billions of trillions of stars are visible to the human eye. Th The luminous spots we see in the sky are all light-years away from Earth. Think of this, there are millions of galaxies in the universe and in each and every one of them, there are billions and trillions of stars. The actual lifespan of a star is highly dependent on its size. There are also 7 classification of stars with 'O' being the massive and hottest while 'M' being the smallest and coolest.
