About Us

We are but a small humble team of two students who are merely interested in the field of astronomy and astrophysics. Both of us are really fascinated and intrigued about the many topics and mysteries of astronomy and what lies beyond us. Because of all of this we created this website to hopefully not only guide ourselves but guide many of you. :)

Astrinity

Astrinity is a website all about any astronomy and anything related to stars, planets, and galaxies. The purpose of this website is for us to record, document, classify the many different celestial bodies, popular or not, in our universe. Here you can find many, many, many celestial bodies that far surpasses or our own planet. Although our ever-expanding universe is a mystery it is up to us to help and guide you uncover these mysteries far beyond our scope. After all, what lies beyond infinity is the greatest mystery.
